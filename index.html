TOP FIVE 311 COMPLAINTS IN EAST NEW YORK

By Jeff Winter

October 3, 2020

East New York is a Brooklyn neighborhood too often reduced to the violence within it. To be sure, it has seen 23 murders in 2020 – up from 9 in 2019 – and shootings are up 80% compared to last year.

But East New York deals with the same everyday frustrations as the rest of the city.

To illustrate those concerns, here are the Top Five 311 Complaints from East New York so far this year:

  • 8, 295 Loud Music and Party Complaints

  • 2, 771 No Access or Blocked Driveway Complaints

  • 1, 341 Blocked Hydrant Complaints

  • 1, 102 Truck and Car Music Complaints

  • 1, 087 Derelict Vehicle Complaints

Source: NYC OpenData
